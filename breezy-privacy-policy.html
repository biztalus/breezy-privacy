Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Breezy ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide:

  • Financial profile data (income, deductions, filing status)
  • Equity compensation details (grants, vesting schedules, exercise history)
  • Employer information
  • Contact information (only if you choose to contact us)

b) Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Social Security numbers
  • Bank account numbers
  • Brokerage account credentials
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking

2. How Your Data is Stored

LOCAL STORAGE ONLY:

All financial data you enter is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it or contact us with your portfolio attached.

ENCRYPTION:

Data stored on your device is protected by iOS Data Protection, which encrypts data when your device is locked.

NO CLOUD SYNC:

The App does not sync data to any cloud service. Your data exists only on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Perform tax calculations and projections within the App
  • Display your equity holdings and financial profile
  • Generate strategy recommendations

We do NOT use your data to:

  • Sell to third parties
  • Target advertisements
  • Build user profiles
  • Train machine learning models
  • Any purpose other than App functionality

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with any third parties, except:

a) At Your Request:

If you use the "Send Feedback" feature and choose to include your portfolio data, that information will be sent to us via email to help address your inquiry.

b) Legal Requirements:

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.

5. Third-Party Services

a) Stock Price Data:

The App fetches current stock prices from Yahoo Finance. Only ticker symbols are transmitted; no personal data is shared.

b) No Analytics:

We do not use any analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.).

c) No Advertising:

The App contains no advertisements and no ad tracking.

6. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. You can delete all data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings.

If you delete the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data stored in the App
  • Export your data (Settings → Send Feedback → include portfolio attachment)
  • Delete all your data (Settings → Clear All Data)
  • Uninstall the App and remove all data

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident:

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not share your personal information for targeted advertising
  • You may request deletion of your data (use Clear All Data in Settings)

10. International Users

The App is designed for U.S. tax calculations. If you use the App outside the United States, you do so at your own risk and are responsible for compliance with local laws.

11. Security

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • Local-only data storage (no transmission)
  • iOS Data Protection encryption
  • No collection of sensitive identifiers

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns, use the "Send Feedback" feature in the app's Settings.
